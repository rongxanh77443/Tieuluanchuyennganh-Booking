Departure: Ho Chi Minh

Destination: Ha Noi

Hanoi is the capital of Vietnam. It covers an area of 3,328.9 square kilometres. With an estimated population of 7.7 million as of 2018, it is the second largest city in Vietnam. The metropolitan area, encompassing nine additional neighbouring provinces, has an estimated population of 16 million. Located in the central area of the Red River Delta, Hanoi is the commercial, cultural, and educational centre of Northern Vietnam.

Departure: Ho Chi Minh

Destination: Vung Tau

Vung Tau is the largest city and former capital of Ba Ria-Vung Tau Province in Vietnam. The city area is 140 square kilometres (54 square miles), consists of 13 urban wards and one commune of Long Son Islet. Vung Tau was the capital of the province until it was replaced by the much smaller Bà Rịa city on 2 May 2012. The city is also the crude oil extraction center of Vietnam.
